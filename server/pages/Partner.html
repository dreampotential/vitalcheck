Executive order 14042 “Ensuring Adequate COVID Safety Protocols for Federal Contractors.”

The Safer Federal Workforce Task Force issued guidance on September 24 detailing the requirements that covered federal contractors and subcontractors must follow to comply with Executive Order 14042, “Ensuring Adequate COVID Safety Protocols for Federal Contractors.”

Covered contractors and subcontractors have until December 8 to ensure that covered employees -including those working from home- are fully vaccinated or qualify for a medical or religious accommodation.
